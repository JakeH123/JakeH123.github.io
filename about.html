About Collecting

Sports Card Investor

One of the premiere places to look for card collecting knowledge would be the Sports Card Investor. Here he explains some of the basic things that you may want to know before collecting cards. Along with knowing these tips, it is also important to know about potential card shows.

Card Shows

These shows are a sort of expo where people go to buy, sell, and trade cards of various types. This is a great place to find hard to get cards or find deals on cards you may be looking for. Along with this it is a great place to meet new people with the same hobby.

By Jake Hoffman
