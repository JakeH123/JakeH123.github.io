Tools

Ebay

Ebay is easily one of the best places to look for cards. It is the most popular user marketplace you can find and constantly has more cards being added. Along with this ebay recently added a verification process for expensive cards so that if you end up spending a lot for a card then you know it will be authentic.

Ebay.com

Market Movers

Market Movers is a great app for any sports card need. This app helps to track past sales, current listings, and market trends for specific cards. Along with this, you can also view cards that are popular at the time. This tool helps to manage what you may want to buy and where to get it.

MarketMoversApp.com

Card Genie Pro

While not essential, Card Genie helps to understand what you have inside of your collection. You can do this by uploading your collection and it will help to sort what you have and what you may need to finish a set. This is not essential to many collectors but can definitely help if you have a lot of cards.

CardGeniePro.com

Amazon

Lastly, a very important tool that you should use would be amazon. This is not specifically to buy cards but is used to buy supplies. These supplies are things such as card sleeves, top loaders, card binders, and more. Using Amazon to directly buy cards is not advised as it is usually overpriced but you may be able to find a deal every once in a while.

Amazon.com
